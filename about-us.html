About Us

Welcome to DocNow - your trusted partner in accessing timely, efficient, and high-quality healthcare services. At DocNow, we believe that healthcare should be accessible and convenient for everyone, and our platform connects patients with professional doctors and specialists quickly and seamlessly.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly, reliable, and secure solution that allows individuals to find, schedule, and receive medical care without the hassle of long wait times.

Our team is committed to innovation and excellence, focusing on delivering healthcare solutions that make a difference. We work with healthcare providers and clinics across multiple cities to ensure you receive the care you need when you need it. DocNow prioritizes your well-being, empowering you to take charge of your health with confidence.

Privacy Policy

At DocNow, protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform:

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect necessary information, such as your personal details, medical records, and device data, to provide you with an optimal experience on our platform. This information is used solely for providing services, such as connecting you with healthcare providers, scheduling appointments, and ensuring the accuracy of your medical records.

2. Data Security

DocNow employs robust security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely. We continually update our security protocols to maintain the highest standards of data protection.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties without your consent, except when required by law or when necessary to provide our services, such as connecting you with a healthcare provider.

4. User Control

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to make any changes to your account, please contact our support team.

5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please reach out to us at support@docnow.com. We are here to address any inquiries and ensure a transparent and safe experience on our platform.
